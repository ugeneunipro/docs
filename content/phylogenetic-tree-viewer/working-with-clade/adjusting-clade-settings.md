# Adjusting Clade Settings

When a clade is selected, the [_branch_](/phylogenetic-tree-viewer/tree-settings/adjusting-branch-settings/) and the [_labels formatting_](/phylogenetic-tree-viewer/tree-settings/modifying-labels-appearance/changing-labels-formatting/) settings are applied to the clade only.

Note that the settings are not applied to the [_collapsed_](/phylogenetic-tree-viewer/working-with-clade/collapsing-and-expanding-branches/) branches (if any).
